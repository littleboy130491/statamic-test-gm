Statamic **collections** work like WordPress custom post types. Attach **taxonomies** on the collection config to get category fields on every entry.

## Key concepts

- **Pages** — static, hierarchical content (like WordPress pages)
- **Posts** — dated entries with categories (like WordPress posts)
- **Categories** — taxonomy terms you assign to posts

Browse posts by category from the blog sidebar or visit a category archive directly.
